Customer Portals

Provide a dedicated hub for your customers to review their requests and engage with the community.




Broker

Broker is the customer portal that allows you to showcase your products to specific customers as well as display pertenant information for the individual customer from contracts to inventory levels.

Tap Room

Tap Room is our dedicated brewery software that helps breweries manage their customers. Provding you with control over what individual customers can see in the portal through an admin dashboard. It is the best way to show buyers what you are brewing.

About

CRM Service Lab is dedicated to providing software that creates transparency between you and your customers. We believe that through transparency, we can eliminate miscommunication and create trust between businesses and customers.

Customer Portals are defined as service and information portals where customers can find information to serve themselves. From support requests to transaction histories, increasing information flow and helping you help your customers.
